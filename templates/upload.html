To upload a group chat, go into the group chat and click the info button, scroll down and choose the email conversation button. Be sure to choose "without media". Email the .txt file to yourself and then upload it here.

To upload a conversation with an individual, go into the conversation and click on the avatar of the person you're chatting to, scroll down and choose the email conversation button. Be sure to choose "without media". Email the .txt file to yourself and then upload it here.
